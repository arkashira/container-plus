```markdown
# Customer Journey Map

## Phase 1: Aware
**Trigger Event**: User encounters issues with basic open-source container tools (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes) and seeks solutions.
**Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about advanced container management solutions, information overload from multiple sources.
**User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, urgency to resolve issues.
**Opportunities to Delight**: Provide clear, concise educational content (blogs, webinars, tutorials) that highlights the pain points and how Container Plus can help.
**Metric**: Number of unique visitors to educational content.

## Phase 2: Consider
**Trigger Event**: User identifies Container Plus as a potential solution during research.
**Friction Points**: Comparison with other solutions, skepticism about the value of paid upgrades, lack of clear ROI.
**User Emotions**: Hopeful but cautious, seeking validation.
**Opportunities to Delight**: Offer free trials, case studies, and testimonials from similar users. Provide a detailed feature comparison with other solutions.
**Metric**: Number of users who sign up for free trials or request demos.

## Phase 3: Try
**Trigger Event**: User signs up for a free trial or demo of Container Plus.
**Friction Points**: Complex onboarding process, technical issues during setup, lack of immediate visible benefits.
**User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety about potential failure.
**Opportunities to Delight**: Streamline onboarding with guided tutorials, provide immediate value through quick wins, offer 24/7 support during the trial period.
**Metric**: Trial completion rate, time to first value.

## Phase 4: Adopt
**Trigger Event**: User decides to purchase Container Plus after a successful trial.
**Friction Points**: Pricing concerns, fear of vendor lock-in, integration challenges with existing systems.
**User Emotions**: Relief, excitement, but also apprehension about long-term commitment.
**Opportunities to Delight**: Offer flexible pricing plans, provide seamless integration support, and highlight success stories of long-term users.
**Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription, customer satisfaction scores.

## Phase 5: Expand
**Trigger Event**: User experiences significant benefits and considers expanding usage within their organization.
**Friction Points**: Budget constraints, resistance to change from other team members, need for additional features.
**User Emotions**: Satisfaction, desire to maximize ROI, eagerness to share success.
**Opportunities to Delight**: Offer upsell opportunities for advanced features, provide training and certification programs, and facilitate user communities for peer support.
**Metric**: Number of additional licenses purchased, expansion revenue.
```
